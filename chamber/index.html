Welcome to Baguio Chamber of Commerce and Industry

About

Under the direction of the Philippine Chamber of Commerce, the Baguio Chamber of Commerce is a voluntary organization of businesses and professionals who have joined together for the purpose of promoting the civic and commercial progress of the City of Baguio City

Vision

To be the engine of business growth in the city geared towards global competitiveness

Mission

To strengthen the capabilities of the members through advocacy, innovation and technology
