Join Us

Becoming a member of the Baguio Chamber of Commerce and Industry is simple. Just fill out our member registration form below and a chamber staff will follow up with you.





















Membership Levels and Benefits

Non-profit

$100 / year
Visibility
  • Business name and description, website, and social media links on our website directory
  • Invitation to participate in Annual Business Expo
  • Half price on standard booth rates at Chamber events
  • Membership certificate, window sticker, and digital chamber member artwork rights
  • Welcome in Chamber’s e-newsletter and on Facebook page
  • Post your events on the Chamber’s calendar of activities
Connections
  • More than 100 annual events where you can interact with other members
  • Invitation to a new member event in your first year
  • Quarterly multi-chamber events where you can network with members from other regional chambers
  • Invitation to quarterly meeting with local city officials
Resources
  • Member rates for all Chamber events for your representative
  • Monthly e-newsletter with Chamber events and community news
  • Advocacy for business interests at local, regional, and nationwide levels
  • Referrals from Chamber staff and volunteers
  • Eligible to participate in leads groups (subject to group requirements)

Bronze

$200 / year
Additional benefits include
  • Ribbon-Cutting or grand opening event promotion and assistance
  • Eligible to host Business After Hours events and sponsor other signature Chamber events
  • Eligible to place ads in the e-newsletter
  • List “Hot Deals” on Chamber website
  • List “Job Openings” on Chamber website
  • Meeting with Chamber staff to strategize around Chamber resources and opportunities
  • One-time use email list or mailing labels for your marketing purposes
  • Free inclusion of your marketing materials in newsletter

Silver

$400 / year
Additional benefits include
  • Featured once annually on Chamber Facebook page, boosted to 20,000+ viewers in surrounding area
  • Free table at annual Business Expo
  • Ten free tickets to Annual Awards event
  • Plaque recognizing Gold Investor Level, appropriate for office or retail display
  • Free standard booth at Chamber events

Gold

$1000 / year
Additional benefits include
  • Company logo featured in annual ad in BaguioBiz
  • Plaque recognizing Platinum Investor Level, appropriate for office or retail display
  • Twenty free tickets to Annual Awards event
  • Annual feature in Chamber’s e-newsletter
  • Logo appears on Chamber website home page, banners
  • Annual meeting with Executive Director and Board Officer
  • Digital “Premium Member” digital artwork for advertising
  • Opportunity to present one award at the Annual Awards event
  • Free PREMIUM booth at Chamber events
  • First crack at sponsorships for Chamber’s signature events
  • Featured once annually on Chamber Facebook page, boosted to 50,000+ viewers in the surrounding area
